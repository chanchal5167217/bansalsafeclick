Guidelines for Quiz:

  • Individual entries are allowed for quiz competition.
  • Each Candidate has to bring Mobile phone for participating in quiz competition.
  • Platform used for quiz competition is slido.com.
  • The questions shall be in the form of multiple choices.
  • Audience/Supporters shall not give any hints or clues to the competitors.
  • Question is available in English only.
  • Total No. of Questions is 15.
  • Each candidate has 30 seconds to solve one question.
  • Overall Time limit including interaction is 30 minutes.
  • Each question will carry one mark.
  • Five Candidates will be selected for Certificates & trophies.
  • In case of tie, Decision is based on quick response to solve the each question.

‘Safe Click’ Drive Launched To Control Cyber Crimes In Madhya Pradesh

With an objective to raise cyber awareness among people, Madhya Pradesh Police has launched a state-wide special awareness drive ‘safe click’ on Saturday which will conclude on February 11 on the occasion of Safer Internet Day. Under the programme, police will raise awareness among internet users and bank customers not only in urban areas but even in remote rural areas of the state.
